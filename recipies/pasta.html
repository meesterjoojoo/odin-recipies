Lemon Parmesan Pasta

Description

My take on buttered noodles has two surprising ingredients that take it over the top. Add cooked broccoli and chicken for a complete meal.

Ingrediants

  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 4 ounces pasta>
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • >1 teaspoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Direction's

  1. STEP 1: Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add salt and pasta and cook until pasta is tender with a bite, about 10 minutes. Drain.
  2. STEP 2: Return pasta to the pot; stir in butter, soy sauce, lemon juice, and Parmesan cheese until creamy. Serve immediately.
